The Founder

Hi, I'm Varun.

Serenity was created as a quiet space in a world that rarely slows down. What began as a personal yoga and meditation practice slowly became something deeper — a way of returning to stillness, clarity, and presence in everyday life.

A Different Approach to Practice

In many places, wellness is treated as another thing to achieve or optimize.

Serenity is different.

Here, the intention is simple: to create space for the body to move, the breath to deepen, and the mind to settle. Through yoga, breathwork, and meditation, these practices become a way to reconnect with yourself — physically, mentally, and emotionally.

Simple Practices. Real Transformation.

You don't need extreme routines or complicated rituals. Often, a few minutes of conscious breathing, mindful movement, or quiet meditation can completely change how the body feels and how the mind responds to the day.

Serenity is a place to explore these practices in a calm, accessible way.

An Invitation

If you're looking for a space to slow down, breathe deeply, and return to a sense of balance, you're welcome here.
